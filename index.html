Career Support Hub

Actionable frameworks to punch above your weight class.

  • The Output Rule: Don't just list responsibilities. Document your operational tradeoffs and the direct business outcomes you drove.
  • Bypassing the ATS Black Hole: Cold emailing founders with a Loom video reviewing their product or funnel yields a 40% higher interview rate.
  • The Compensation Fallacy: Base salary is a vanity metric. Evaluate roles strictly on learning velocity, network density, and equity upside.
  • AI-Augmented Execution: The new baseline isn't knowing a specific software; it's leveraging AI to execute projects 5x faster than a traditional professional.

Company Hiring Insights

Hard truths on hyper-scaling your core team.

  • The Kerala Arbitrage: Sourcing from South India isn't just about cost. It's about zero-entitlement, high-grit professionals who execute significantly faster.
  • The Conversion Trial: Stop doing endless theoretical interviews. Pay candidates for a weekend project or brief simulation to get a 90% higher signal-to-noise ratio.
  • Retention Through Autonomy: Top performers despise micromanagement. They demand a clear problem statement and ownership of the solution.
  • Asynchronous Protocol: The best global teams don't align on daily meetings. They operate on strict, written-first asynchronous workflows.
