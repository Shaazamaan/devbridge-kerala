Who Is This

A Personal Effort.
Not an Agency.

I started DevBridge Kerala because I saw people around me — people I genuinely care about — struggling to find the right jobs, connections, or even just a clear direction. This is not a business or a recruitment firm. It's me, personally, trying to help where I can.

If you're from my network — or know someone from Kerala who's struggling — reach out. I'll do my best. Whether it's a job in Dubai, an investor intro, a pitch deck, or just figuring out the next step.
